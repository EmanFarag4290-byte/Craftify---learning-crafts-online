# Craftify 🎨🔨

Craftify is an interactive, multi-lingual (English & Arabic) web application dedicated to discovering, learning, and mastering handmade skills. Designed with an elegant, artisan-inspired aesthetic, it provides users with tools to stay inspired, learn through tailored content, plan their projects, and manage their focus.

## 🚀 Features

* **Bilingual Experience:** Full support for both **English** and **Arabic** (RTL support) interfaces.
* **Craft Discovery:** Interactive discovery engine highlighting various crafts like Woodworking, Pottery, Embroidery, and Leatherworking.
* **Curated Video Playlists:** Dynamically loaded YouTube playlist integrations for step-by-step video learning.
* **Project Planner:** A built-in Kanban/Task planner to help artisans sketch out, track, and execute their craft projects.
* **Craft-focused Pomodoro Timer:** A specialized productivity timer with adjustable focus intervals to prevent burnout during intense crafting sessions.
* **Community Gallery:** A showcase space for crafters to upload and share images of their completed works.
* **Artisan Shop:** A beautiful marketplace UI showcasing handmade products with functional filtering, dynamic item search, and a live cart calculator.

## 🎨 Design & Theme

Craftify utilizes an organic, grounded color palette designed to mirror raw crafting materials:
* 🧱 **Terracotta** (`#b3562d`) — Primary accent, symbolizing clay and warmth.
* 🌾 **Sage** (`#7a9e87`) — Secondary accent, bringing a natural feel.
* 🍦 **Cream & Ink** — For soft, high-contrast, readable typography.
* 📜 **Typography:** Features a premium pairing of *Cormorant Garamond* for artistic headers and *DM Sans* for crisp interface readability.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** Semantic HTML5, CSS3 (Custom properties, Grid, Flexbox)
* **Interactivity:** Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
* **Fonts:** Google Fonts (*Cormorant Garamond*, *DM Sans*, *Dancing Script*)
